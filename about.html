🧪 Who We Are

About Trail Built

Honest gear reviews and build guides from overlanders who actually use this stuff.

Our Mission

Trail Built exists because most gear "reviews" online are just repackaged spec sheets and Amazon listings. We got tired of reading about gear that nobody had actually used, so we started testing everything ourselves — on real trails, in real conditions, across real miles.

Since 2023, we've driven over 50,000 miles testing overlanding gear, from recovery kits in the Mojave to rooftop tents in the Cascades. Every product recommendation on this site reflects first-hand experience or rigorous research from trusted sources in the overlanding community.

Editorial Standards

We don't accept free products from manufacturers in exchange for reviews. We don't let affiliate relationships influence our ratings. If something breaks on the trail, we say so. Our goal is to help you make the right gear decision — not to maximize clicks.

  • We test before we recommend. Products featured as top picks have been field-tested or researched extensively.
  • We update our reviews. Gear changes, new models ship, prices shift — we revisit articles regularly.
  • We're transparent about limits. If we haven't tested something personally, we say so and explain our research methodology.

Affiliate Disclosure

Trail Built participates in the Amazon Associates program and other affiliate programs. When you click a product link and make a purchase, we may earn a small commission at no extra cost to you. These commissions help keep the lights on so we can keep publishing free, independent reviews.

Our affiliate relationships never influence which products we recommend. We link to gear because it's good — not because it pays more. You'll find honest assessments even when that means steering you away from high-commission products.

Privacy Policy

Trail Built does not collect personal information beyond standard web analytics (page views, general traffic sources). We use this data only to understand which content is most useful to our readers.

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties. Amazon and other affiliate partners may set cookies in accordance with their own privacy policies when you click affiliate links.

This site does not require account creation and does not store user data.
