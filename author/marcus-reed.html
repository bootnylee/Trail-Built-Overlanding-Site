Marcus Reed

Lead Gear Editor — Trail Built

Marcus is the Lead Gear Editor at Trail Built. His focus is the hardware that determines whether you get home or spend the night on the trail: recovery gear, winches, lighting, and suspension. He evaluates products by putting them through the conditions they were designed for — not by reading spec sheets. When a product fails, he says so.

His reviews cover the full range of overlanding equipment, with particular depth on recovery systems and vehicle build components for mid-size trucks and SUVs.
