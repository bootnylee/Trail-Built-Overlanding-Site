FAQ

What is the primary purpose of a skid plate?

A skid plate is designed to protect the underside of your vehicle, particularly the engine, transmission, and fuel tank, from damage caused by rocks, branches, and other obstacles encountered during off-road driving.

How do I choose the right skid plate for my off-road truck?

When selecting a skid plate, consider factors such as the type of off-road driving you'll be doing, the make and model of your vehicle, and your budget. Look for skid plates made from high-quality materials, such as thick steel, and ensure they are compatible with your vehicle.

Can I install a skid plate myself, or do I need to hire a professional?

While it's possible to install a skid plate yourself, we recommend hiring a professional mechanic if you're not experienced with vehicle modifications. Improper installation can lead to reduced protection and potentially cause damage to your vehicle.
