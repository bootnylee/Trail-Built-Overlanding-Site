FAQ

What is the best way to conserve water while overlanding?

Conserving water is essential while overlanding, as access to clean water may be limited. We recommend using low-flow showerheads, taking shorter showers, and using waterless hygiene products whenever possible.

How do I choose the right shower and hygiene solution for my overlanding needs?

Choosing the right shower and hygiene solution depends on several factors, including the length and type of your trip, the number of people in your group, and your personal preferences. Consider the products we've recommended above, and think about your specific needs and budget.

Can I use a portable shower system in cold weather?

Yes, many portable shower systems are designed to work in cold weather, but it's essential to take some precautions. Insulate your water tank and hoses, and consider using a heated shower system or a shower with a built-in water heater.
