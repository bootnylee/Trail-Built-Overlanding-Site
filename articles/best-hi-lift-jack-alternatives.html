FAQ

What is the weight capacity of a typical hi-lift jack?

A typical hi-lift jack has a weight capacity of around 7,000 pounds (3,175 kg). However, some heavy-duty models can have a weight capacity of up to 20 tons (44,000 pounds or 19,958 kg).

Can I use a hi-lift jack on a paved road?

While it's technically possible to use a hi-lift jack on a paved road, it's not recommended. Hi-lift jacks are designed for off-road use, and the rough surface can damage the jack or your vehicle. If you need to change a tire on a paved road, it's best to use a standard floor jack or a scissor jack.

How do I maintain my hi-lift jack to ensure it lasts long?

To maintain your hi-lift jack, make sure to regularly lubricate the moving parts, clean the jack after each use, and store it in a dry place. You should also inspect the jack for damage or wear before each use and replace any worn-out parts as needed.
